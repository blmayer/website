homemade recipes

Homemade Recipes or Feita em casa is an Android app published on the Google Play Store, it features an intelligent search of recipes by ingredient. The ideia is to help people use the ingredients they have at home, not the opposite, as it is commonly seen, so people can cook without leaving home to buy food.

Currently it is available on 4 countries: Brazil, Portugal, United States and United Kingdom. Recipes are different depending on the language. The iOS app is comming soon.
